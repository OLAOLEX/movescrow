How Escrow Works

Simple, secure, and transparent transaction process

1

Initiate Transaction

Buyer initiates a transaction and funds are securely deposited into the escrow account. Payment is verified and held safely.

2

Service Completion

Seller completes the service as agreed. Buyer verifies completion and satisfaction before authorizing fund release.

3

Fund Release
